Season Summary

In the second year of this fantasy football league, the league made the decision to convert from nfl.com to ESPN. However not much data exists from this year as ESPN seemingly purged their pre-2016 fanasty football league data. The only data accurate data from this year is the draft data. The manager names on the draft board have been substituted with vague but still identifiable names because the actual draft board maintained managers real names and the discussion on using real names has not occured at the time of this paragraph.

The rules data included are the 2015 ESPN because presumably the precocious league did not understand the complexities of diverging from the defaults. If the 2015 fantasy data is gathered, this page and corresponding pages will be populated.
